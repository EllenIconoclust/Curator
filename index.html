Deep Breathing

Journaling
Write down your thoughts without filtering them. Let your emotions flow out safely.
Body Scan Meditation
Focus on each part of your body from head to toe, noticing tension and relaxing it.
Gratitude Exercise
List 3 things you're grateful for today and reflect on why they matter to you.
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Helps with anxiety.
Progressive Muscle Relaxation
Tense and relax each muscle group from toes to head, holding for 5 seconds each.
Loving-Kindness Meditation
Repeat phrases like "May I be happy, may I be healthy" for yourself and others.
Power Pose
Stand like a superhero (hands on hips) for 2 minutes to reduce stress hormones.
Dance Break
Play one upbeat song and move freely to boost your mood.
